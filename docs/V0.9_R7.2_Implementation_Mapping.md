## Authority Model

- `WorkflowOperationLock` is the cross-process mutation boundary.
- `RuntimeState` is intent and progress, not independent resource truth.
- Recovery Journal plus current process/display observations establish managed ownership.
- Clean Normal is authoritative only for Normal readiness and Enable eligibility.
- Operational Evidence is ephemeral, typed, read-only evidence for non-Normal presentation.
- The temporary uninstall coordinator is trusted by a SHA-256 verified byte copy of a trusted CLI in a private `0700` directory.

R7.2 defines no separate P2 remediation item. No source-level P2 was identified by the final audit.

## Failure Scenarios

- [ ] Enable immediately after cooldown.
- [ ] Enable under high CPU load.
- [ ] Enable with a large process table.
- [ ] Request Restore immediately after Enable.
- [ ] Let confirmation timeout roll back.
- [ ] Relaunch App during Confirm Required.
- [ ] Inject helper launch failure and verify one compensation pass.
- [ ] Inject authorization timeout and verify response under 500ms.
- [ ] Verify failed helper diagnostics contain one complete `[Helper]` line.
- [ ] Verify no unprefixed/truncated fragment exists in the log.

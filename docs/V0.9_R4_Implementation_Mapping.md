## Current System Invariants

- RuntimeState is a projection, not a resource trust root.
- Recovery Journal is the independent authority during incomplete work and may not be removed while a resource or stale state can remain.
- One workflow lock protects Core Enable/Restore/config decisions across App and CLI.
- Display handoff persists evidence before topology changes; a replacement remains until physical takeover and required restoration are verified.
- Brightness fallback is prohibited when original brightness cannot be reliably read and restored.
- Keep Awake PID is the direct IOPM assertion holder and requires complete ownership verification.
- Restore is resumable through cleanup progress; unknown stages are re-observed.
- Helper side effects require a one-time workflow capability.
- Final success requires observed Clean Normal, not merely Mode Normal.
